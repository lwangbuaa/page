About me

I am currently an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science, Beihang University (BUAA), China. I received my PhD degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) , supervised by Prof. Terrence Mak and Prof. Ho-fung Leung. Prior to joining BUAA, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, collaborating with Prof. Leibo Liu and Prof. Shaojun Wei.

My research focuses on computer architecture, including AI accelerators, on-chip/chiplet interconnects, GPU architecture, and hardware acceleration. I have published over 30 papers in top-tier conferences and journals, including ISCA, MICRO, HPCA, DAC, DATE, IEEE TC, IEEE TPDS, IEEE TCAD, JSA, etc. I have received the best paper award in VLSI-SoC and DSD conferences.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • Point cloud accelerators

  • LLM quantization and acceleration

  • GPU architecture

  • Network-on-chip

  • Chiplet architecture

  • Reconfigurable computing
